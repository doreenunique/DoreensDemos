About Us

Our History

In the heart of the vibrant city of Athens, nestled between the rhythm of the streets and the pulse of cultural diversity, "Hair Dresser Stylish and My Curls" emerged as a beacon of celebration for Afro-hair and curly hair styling.

Founded in 2013, by two sisters, sofia and irene ukpebor,both with a passion for embracing natural textures and unlocking the beauty of every curl, the salon was born out of a desire to create a space where individuals could celebrate the uniqueness of their hair.

The two sisters, a visionary in the world of hairdressing, grew up surrounded by the rich tapestry of Afro-centric styles and traditions. With a deep understanding of the diverse hair textures and a keen eye for trends, she envisioned a salon that would not only offer top-notch styling but also be a haven for self-expression and empowerment.

From its humble beginnings as a small boutique salon, Hair Dresser Stylish and My Curls quickly became a community hub, attracting a clientele seeking expertise in Afro and curly hair care. Olivia's commitment to education and embracing the beauty of natural hair led to the salon becoming a trusted space for those transitioning from chemical treatments to embracing their curls.

Over the years, the salon has evolved into a cultural hub, hosting events that celebrate Afro and curly hair artistry. From natural hair expos to workshops on self-care and styling, Hair Dresser Stylish and My Curls has been at the forefront of promoting confidence and pride in natural hair.

sofia and irene's dedication to sustainability is reflected in the salon's use of eco-friendly and cruelty-free hair products, reinforcing the commitment to both the beauty of natural hair and environmental consciousness.

As the salon continues to thrive, it stands as a testament to Olivia's vision of creating a space where every curl is celebrated, and every individual feels confident, stylish, and uniquely themselves. Hair Dresser Stylish and My Curls is not just a salon; it's a movement, a celebration of authenticity, and a home for those who want to embrace the beauty of their natural hair journey..
